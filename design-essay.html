Marian Bantjes is a designer, typographer, writer and illustrator. She started out as an art student at Emily Carr College of Art, but Marian dropped out of art school early and decided to train as a typesetter in Canada. It was here that Marian learnt a lot about typography which lead to her career in design. Marian Banjtes work is illustrative which makes it so unique. Her most famous works include ‘Speak-Up’ t-shirt design and the book ‘I wonder’.
